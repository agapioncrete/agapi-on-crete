About Villa Agapi

Nestled in the scenic hills of Episkopi, Crete, Villa Agapi offers breathtaking sea views, a private swimming pool, and stylish modern interiors. This elegant retreat is perfect for families or couples looking to escape into serenity and comfort. Enjoy the charm of Mediterranean design, a fully equipped kitchen, sun-drenched terraces, and proximity to local tavernas and pristine beaches.

Whether you're watching the sunset over the sea or enjoying a quiet morning coffee surrounded by olive trees, Villa Agapi invites you to relax, recharge, and reconnect with nature.
